Story by Devil Barewar

KULHAD BREAK - FULL SCRIPT

EXT. TAPARI, MORNING

Ek ladka aur ladki haath mein kulhad pakadkar ek dusre se lad
rahe hote hai. Aur ladki ladke se break-up karti hai. Aur
ladki ke haath se kulhad chhutkar niche zameen par girta hai.
Tute hue kulhad ke upar naam likhkar aata hai - Hrival
Creation Presents Kulhad Break

CUT TO:
EXT. TAPARI, NIGHT

(Bg noise)
Ek ladka do kulhad mein chai lakar deta hai. Ladka dono
kulhad uthakar apne baju mein table rakhta hai.

(Back shot)
Ladka ek kulhad uthata hai aur chai ki ek chuski leta hai.
Woh dard mein hota hai.

(OV voice)
Tum aaj mere bina chai pee rahe ho.

Ladka baju mein dekhta hai. Uski gf hoti hai, jisse uska
break-up hua hota hai. Ladka use dekhta hai aur phir chai
peene lagta hai.

(Front shot)
Ladka khada hota hai aur wahan se chala jata hai. Dusre wale
kulhad mein ab bhi chai waisi ki waisi hoti hai.

NEXT DAY
EXT. TAPARI, NIGHT

Ladka phir usi jagah par baithata hai. Woh do kulhad leta hai
aur dono ko apne baju mein rakhkar chai peeta hai aur wahan
se chala jata hai. Dusre kulhad mein chai waisi ki waisi hi
hoti hai.

NEXT DAY
EXT. TAPARI, NIGHT

Ladka do kulhad leta hai aur apne baju mein rakhta hai. Woh
chai pee raha hota hai, tabhi uske baju mein ek ladki aakar
baithati hai aur uske purse ke jhatke se table par rakhi
kulhad niche girti hai.

(Ground shot)
Kulhad tutti hai.

(Bg shot)
Ladka kulhad ki taraf dekhta hai. Phir baju mein ladki ko
dekhta hai. Ladki muskura deti hai.
