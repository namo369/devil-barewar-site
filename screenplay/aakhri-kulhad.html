Ek table par do kulhad rakhi hoti hai. Ek waiter aakar usme
se ek kulhad uthata hi hai aur woh dusri kulhad wahin
chhodkar chala jata hai.

Us bachi kulhad ke baju me naam likhkar aata hai - Hrival
