EXT. TAPARI, DAY

Ek ladki tapari mein, Ek table par baithkar ek kitab padhte
hue kulhad se chai pee rahi hai. Tabhi uske mobile par call
aata hai. Woh kulhad aur kitab table par chhodkar wahan se
uthkar door jakar baat Karti hai. Aur achnak daudte hue bahar
chali jati hai.

Table par rakhi kitab aur kulhad ke baju mein naam likhkar
aata hai - Hrival Creation Presents Gumshuda Kulhad

EXT. TAPARI, DAY

Wahin baju wali table par baitha ladka us kitab aur kulhad ki
taraf dekhta hai. Woh apne jagah se uthata hai. Aur kitab
lekar bahar ki taraf jata hai. Lekin wahan woh ladki nahi
hoti. Ladka kitab ko lekar tapari mein aa jata hai.

INT. HOME, NIGHT

Ladka andar wale kamare se hall mein aata hai. Tea table par
usi ladki ki kitab ko dekhta hai.

KRISH
Kal jakar us ladki ko kitab deni
padegi, lekin usse pahle main
padhkar dekhta hun.

Ladka kitab ke panne paltata hai. Phir sochta hai, kyu na
kitab ke saath ek cup chai ho jae. Ladka kitchen me jakar ek
chai banata hai. Use woh cup me chhankar hall mein sofe par
aakar baithata hai aur cup se ek guth chai peekar cup ko tea
table par rakhta hai. Woh kitab ko uthakar use padhne wala
hota hai ki usme se ek kagaz niche girta hai. Ladka kagaz
uthata hai. Usmein likha hota hai - Agar tum yeh kitab padh
rahe ho, toh iska matlab hai ki woh tum tak pahunch chuki
hai.
