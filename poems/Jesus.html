Jesus my lord...

An English prayer-poem asking for light, peace and healing in a dark world.

Jesus my lord... Jesus, My Lord! where are you? The darkness of suffering spread everywhere, The sand of peace turns into the rush of nowhere, The evil needs changes to selfish mere, We lost the way, we lose somewhere. Jesus! what are you waiting for? The tears of pain became the case of rage, The mind of prospering turns into the dark evil cage, Breaths are selling like the diamonds price tag, Even the deaths trending like the social hashtag. Jesus! what are you preparing for? A side the world fighting for live, On another behalf, ego touching the sky, Religion becomes the word of die(dice), The shame of shit in the game of cry. Jesus! your children have forgotten the mean, Starts to afraid in the land of dream, Show the light of spiritual awakening, Help us in this pandemic timing, Save the world, save nature!
