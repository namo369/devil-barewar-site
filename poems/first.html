First meet

An English poem about the very first meeting with a mother.

First meet She is the ocean of love and seashore of care, The sky of support and gratitude of prayer. She was the first person I met in my life, Having beautiful soul with loving rife. She holds me firmly in her protective arm, Just like I was a precious lucky charm. She soothes me with her sweet voice, And sang a silent song of her choice. She whispered something in my ear, I remember, it was a holy prayer. That was my first meet with my mother, Which lives with me forever, for each other...
